I'm member of Living History reenactment group from Slovakia, DS Satyros.

This "hobby" has made a great impact on my life and affected me in many ways. Whether it be camp creation, cooking food or yourself on the sun in armor before or during fight.

Costumes

Great part of medieval reenactment is to have a costume matching to the time period. Not only with its shape and materials, but also color. Great community has been created by reenactment scene, so sharing knowledge or attentind workshops is done on regular basis.
